Artifact Purpose

From car search to decision intelligence

Dealer websites usually sort by price, year, or mileage. This prototype goes further by turning buyer preferences into a decision score and explaining why each vehicle is ranked. It is not a live dealer search engine; it is a responsible decision-support prototype that can evaluate sample vehicles or user-entered real listings.

Base scoring categories

  • Reliability and maintenance risk
  • Resale value and depreciation estimate
  • Mileage, age, accident/title risk

Responsible AI framing

This prototype avoids claiming certainty. It uses explainable, adjustable scoring rather than hidden recommendations. The tool supports human judgment and encourages inspection, test drives, vehicle history checks, and professional pre-purchase inspection before any buying decision.

AutoWise Finder is an educational AIML 500 portfolio artifact and should not be treated as financial, legal, mechanical, or dealership advice.
